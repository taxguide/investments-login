The **Fidelity Investments Login** portal lets you securely access your brokerage, retirement, and investment accounts online. After signing in, you can check your portfolio, manage investments, transfer funds, view account statements, and update your account settings.

This guide covers how to log in, recover your username or password, fix common login issues, and securely access your Fidelity account.

How to Log In to Your Fidelity Investments Account
--------------------------------------------------

#. Visit the official Fidelity login page.
#. Enter your **Username**.
#. Enter your **Password**.
#. Complete any security verification if prompted.
#. Click **Log In**.

Forgot Username or Password?
----------------------------

If you can't access your account:

* Select **Forgot Username** or **Forgot Password**.
* Verify your identity.
* Follow the instructions to recover your account.
* Sign in using your updated credentials.

Common Login Problems
---------------------

You may be unable to sign in because of:

* Incorrect username or password.
* Browser cache issues.
* Outdated browser.
* Internet connection problems.
* Temporary account lock after multiple failed attempts.

Clear your browser cache, try another browser, or reset your password if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

**Can I access my Fidelity account on mobile?**

Yes. You can sign in using the official Fidelity mobile app.

**What if I forget my password?**

Use the **Forgot Password** option on the login page to reset it.

**Is Fidelity Login secure?**

Yes. Fidelity uses encryption and additional security verification to help protect customer accounts.
